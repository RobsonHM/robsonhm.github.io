Coxinha

What it is

Coxinha is a deep-fried savory pastry made from a soft dough (usually wheat flour cooked in chicken broth) and filled with shredded chicken.

It's traditionally shaped like a teardrop, said to resemble a chicken drumstick (coxa in Portuguese—hence the name).

Filling & variations

Classic: Shredded chicken with cream cheese (requeijão)

Chicken with catupiry-style cheese

Beef or pulled pork

Vegetarian versions (mushroom, jackfruit, hearts of palm)

Mini coxinhas for parties (coxinha de festa)
