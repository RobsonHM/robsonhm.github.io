Focus on Responsiveness

This content block demonstrates the power of responsive design classes. Notice how the horizontal padding adapts dramatically when the viewport crosses the 1024px breakpoint, switching from px-8 to lg:px-64.

The font sizes for both the heading and the paragraphs also change based on the screen size, ensuring readability and visual hierarchy across devices, from mobile phones to large desktops.

Spacing and Typography Rules

The vertical rhythm is controlled precisely by the required margin units. The h2 uses mb-2 (0.5rem), providing a snug fit before the content begins, while the paragraphs use mb-4 (1rem).

The tight tracking on the h2, combined with the semibold weight, gives the title a distinct, modern, and impactful appearance, especially at the large text-6xl size.
